Global Windows Game Conference (GWGC) is a branch of the Animation Game Internationalization Working Committee. It is hosted by the Beijing Animation Game Industry Alliance, which is established by companies and developers around the world who are engaged in Microsoft development. It provides a free platform for Microsoft system developers to communicate.

GWGC is committed to bringing excellent games to players around the world. We teamed up with the world's leading Win10 publisher and channel Miracle Games to reach all-platform users worldwide in a win-win situation.
