How to get the points interface display？

Recharge Miracle Beans in any game on the platform, you can get 10 points for every 0.15 dollar recharged;

The validity period of the points is calculated in natural years and two years is an effective period. That is, the points you earn in the first year are valid for use within the validity period of two years. For example: points earned in June 2018 and cleared on December 31, 2019;
